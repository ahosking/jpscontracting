About Us

Our Story

JPS Contracting is a Design-Build company that focuses on architecturally advanced designs and specializes in high-end remodels, home additions and custom homes.
We serve homeowners and landlords all throughout York Region and the Greater Toronto Areas.
JPS Contracting and Renovations provides turn key solutions to homeowners who are interested in embarking on a construction journey of their own. Working hand-in-hand with homeowners to design the project that perfectly fits their family character and style, and delivering that design to them by way of 3D rendering to aid them in the visualization of their upcoming project to ensure it is their dream design prior to commencing.

We focus heavily on the design and ensure it is exactly what the homeowner loves first and foremost. Complete and proper planning is the key to a successful project; more specifically, an efficient execution, staying within project budget and customer satisfaction.

We are fully licensed, insured and professionally trained and skilled in all facets of the trade industry. With over 15 years experience in the renovations, and new build-outs, JPS Contracting is your sure choice to a 100% satisfied turn-key solution. In addition to our residential focus, we do provide light commercial services that do not require structural modifications. Full Design-Build services available.
